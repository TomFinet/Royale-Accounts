FAQ

Can my account be banned?

Yes, but very unlikely. We ensure that all accounts have had an IP cooldown of 24 hours so that Supercell has no way of knowing that an account transfer has occured. If your account is banned, contact us immediately and we will fully refund your order.

How quickly do I receive my account?

Within seconds. After you have payed for an account order, our systems automatically send you a confirmation email and provide your game account access details in the order details page.

How do I know the payment method is secure?

To handle bank payments we use the Stripe payment gateway which is used by Facebook, Pinterest, SquareSpace, Shopify and Lyft. All payments are encrypted in 256-bit over a secure SSL connection. Don't worry, you are in safe hands.

What are the benefits of creating an account?

Creating an account allows us to save your data to make the checkout process as seemless as possible. It also allows you to view all your previous orders and details related to them very easily compared to as if you were a guest.

What data do you save about us?

We store a reference to your payment methods which are stored securely on Stripe servers, saved billing addresses and your email, that's it. We do not share your data with any third parties. What happens on Royale Accounts, stays on Royale Accounts.

Still have questions?

If you are still unsure or have questions, feel free to send us an email on our contact page, our staff are more than happy to help you.
